About Us

Our Loans

We offer direct loans based on your business cash flows and stock. Loans of up to UGX 60,000,000 with minimal requirement like your National ID and legal documents showing ownership of the business.

Our Mission

We started AgriFund to empower Ugandan farmers with fair, accessible loans that help grow their businesses and communities and eventually lifting them out of poverty. AgriFund connects farmers to various banks to enable them acquire loans at reduced interest rates with longer payment periods to expand their farmers from subsistance farming to commercialized farming.
